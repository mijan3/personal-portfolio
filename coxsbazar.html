Cox's Bazar

One of my favorite travel destinations in Bangladesh.

Why I Like Cox's Bazar

Cox's Bazar is one of the most beautiful travel destinations in Bangladesh. It is famous for its long sea beach, peaceful environment, and amazing sunset view.

Main Attractions

  • Longest natural sea beach
  • Beautiful sunset view
  • Fresh air and peaceful environment
  • Great place for vacation and photography
