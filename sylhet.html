Sylhet

A peaceful green travel destination in Bangladesh.

Why I Like Sylhet

Sylhet is one of the most beautiful places in Bangladesh. It is famous for tea gardens, green hills, rivers, and natural beauty. I like Sylhet because it has a calm and peaceful environment.

Main Attractions

  • Beautiful tea gardens
  • Green hills and natural views
  • Peaceful rivers and waterfalls
  • Great place for nature lovers
